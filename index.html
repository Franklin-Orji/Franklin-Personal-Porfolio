Who I Am

What started as a curiosity about how the web works has evolved into a career-defining passion for building it. As a developer, I focus on the 'building blocks'—HTML, CSS, and JS—while leveraging React to create engaging digital experiences. I don't just write code; I design solutions to complex problems and build interfaces that feel intuitive.

I am proactive about learning new technologies and am eager to contribute my dedication, creativity, and fresh perspective to a dynamic development team. I am looking for a challenging junior-level position or internship where I can apply my current technical skills and grow under the mentorship of experienced professionals. I am excited to embark on this career path and make a positive impact in the web development community.

Beyond financial perks, I have strong motivation and drive to turn creative ideas into tangible solutions that solve real-life problems, allowing women to act as innovators rather than just users of technology. I'm drawn to tech because it allows me bring diverse perspectives into product development, particularly in female-oriented fields like femtech, healthcare, and AI ethics. The desire to break gender stereotypes, be part of a forward-thinking community, and lead in a field that actively seeks diversity provides a compelling impetus to take the leap into tech roles.
